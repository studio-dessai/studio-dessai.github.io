Studio d'essai is an hour of electronic and experimental music spanning space and time – with a special interest in Free culture –, on Brisbane's Global digital, presented by Stéphane Guillou and Lochlan Morrissey. Listen live from 10-11 pm Thursday nights (AEST or UTC+10) or stream the latest episode.


You can interact with us on the federated social network:

  • Diaspora: studiodessai@framasphere.org
  • Friendica: studiodessai@friendica.eu
  • GNU Social: studiodessai@quitter.se
  • Pump.io: studiodessai@pumpdog.me
You can also get quick news from us by:
  • Following our Telegram channel: https://telegram.me/studiodessai
  • Subscribing to our RSS feed.

Find all the playlists to date below, as well as some extra info and links, and the audio stream of recent shows. If the licenses of what we play are permissive enough, the show is available to download too. Note that the files are .ogg, so make sure you have software capable of playing such files (e.g. VLC media player or Clementine).
