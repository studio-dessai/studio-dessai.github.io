Studio d'essai is an hour of experimental and electronic music transversing space and time on Brisbane's 4EB Global digital, presented by Stéphane Guillou and Lochlan Morrissey. Listen live online from 10-11 pm Thursday nights (AEST or UTC+10).



You can find us on Diaspora: just search for “studio d'essai” or “studiodessai” and you should be right.
