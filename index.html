Studio d'essai is an hour of electronic and experimental music spanning space and time – with a special interest in the Commons –, on Brisbane's Global digital, created by Stéphane Guillou and Lochlan Morrissey. Listen live from 10-11 pm Thursday nights (AEST or UTC+10), stream the latest episode or add our podcast feed to your favourite podcatcher (try AntennaPod for example).


You can get news about the latest episode by subscribing to our RSS feed.


You can also interact with us on decentralised networks:

  • Chat in our Riot room: #studiodessai:matrix.org
  • Follow our posts on Diaspora: studiodessai@framasphere.org
  • Toot with us on Mastodon: studiodessai@framapiaf.org

Find all the playlists to date below, as well as some extra info and links, and the audio stream of recent shows. If the licenses of what we play are permissive enough, the show is available to download too. You can use free software to play the audio files (e.g. VLC media player or Clementine).
