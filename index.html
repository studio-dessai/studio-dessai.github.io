Studio d'essai is an hour of electronic and experimental music spanning space and time – with a special interest in Free culture –, on Brisbane's Global digital, presented by Stéphane Guillou and Lochlan Morrissey. Listen live from 10-11 pm Thursday nights (AEST or UTC+10), stream the latest episode or add our podcast feed to your favourite podcatcher (try AntennaPod for example).


You can get quick news from us by:

  • Following our Telegram channel: https://telegram.me/studiodessai
  • Subscribing to our RSS feed.

You can also interact with us on the federated social network:

  • Diaspora: studiodessai@framasphere.org
  • Friendica: studiodessai@friendica.eu
  • GNU Social: studiodessai@quitter.se
  • Pump.io: studiodessai@pumpdog.me
